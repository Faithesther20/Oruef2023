Arch Bishop Margaret Benson Idahosa (JP,OON)

Proprietress, Word of Faith School

Theme Educating for Eternity: Transforming lives through the infinite love of Christ

Word of Faith Group of schools was established in pursuit of the vision from our mother and Proprietress Archbishop (Dr.) M.E. Benson Idahosa to create academic enviroment where children are brought up with optimal Christian ideals. This confrence is a bold attempt to train educators, to enable them train up children in the way they should go, so that when they are old they would not depart from it.

Our theme educating for eternity: transforming lives through the infinite love of Christ seeks to train educators to teach every child in love, inculcate an excellent spirit and right type of values to make them God fearing and academically outstanding.

Word of Faith Schools patner with Oral Robert University Education Fellowship (ORUef) and the International Christian Accrediting Association (ICAA) to bring the Annual African Christian Schools Confrence to Africa. The confrence is aimed at training school administrators and teachers to become 21st century christian educators with enhanced skills to be at per with world best practices.

Everything must perish where God's word is not studied increasingly... for if the scriptures and learning perish, what will remain
