Theme: Educating for Eternity:Running with the Vision

Word of faith Group of schools was established in pursuit of the vision from our mother and Proprietress Archbishop (Dr.) M.E. Benson Idahosa to create academic enviroment where children are brought up with optimal Christian ideals. This confrence is a bold attempt to train educators, to enable them train up children in the way they should go. So that when they are old they would not depart from it.

Our theme educating for eternity: running with the vision seeks to train educators to enculcate in every child an excellent spirit, and right type of values, which aspires to make every child God fearing and academically outstanding.

Everything must perish where God's word is not studied increasingly... for if the scriptures and learning perish, what will remain
